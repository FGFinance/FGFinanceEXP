Expense Calculator


The FG Finances Expense Calculator is a simple tool, but in the right hands can be very useful.

Have you ever wondered where all your money goes? Why your bank account is empty after the end of each month? It sounds silly, but the Expense Calculator can help you!
The only thing you have to do is put in your monthly income, fill out all the fields (such as you rent, how much you spend in food, fuel, etc.) and the calculator will do everything else for you! It'll quickly analyze your expenses, highlight the areas of highest deficit and provide a quick solution to what you should do towards it.
And the best part? None of this information leaves your device! Everything is calculated in your browser in a quick and simple way, ensuring your privacy is secure and the only one coming out on top is YOU!
(Please note that the calculator can not know everything about your life. Not all suggestions can be accurate towards your situation. Please seek to examine your expenses on your own to avoid any misinformation.)
